## Why this site exists

Most backend tutorials tell you **what** to type. This site explains **why** —
the tradeoffs behind every choice in a real, production-shaped system. It grew
out of more than **100 real questions** asked while building production backends:
why Kafka over RabbitMQ, when a database lock is actually a transaction, why
analytics lives in PostgreSQL and not MongoDB, how Temporal differs from Celery.

Everything here is free, fast, and searchable. Hit <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>/<kbd>⌘</kbd> +
<kbd>K</kbd> to search any concept instantly.
