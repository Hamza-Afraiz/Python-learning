_Last updated: 3 June 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how **Backend Engineering, Explained** ("we", "us",
"this site") handles information when you visit
`https://hamza-afraiz.github.io/Python-learning/`.

## Summary

This is a free, static educational website. We do not ask you to create an
account, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly. Some
data may be collected automatically by third-party services described below.

## Information we collect

- **No personal accounts.** There is no sign-up, login, or form on this site, so
  we do not collect names, emails, or passwords.
- **Automatic technical data.** Like most websites, our hosting provider
  (GitHub Pages) may automatically log standard technical information such as
  your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit, for security and
  operational purposes.
- **Local browser storage.** The site may store small preferences (such as your
  light/dark theme choice) in your browser. This never leaves your device.

## Cookies and advertising

If advertising is enabled on this site, third-party vendors — including
**Google** — may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or
other websites.

- Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads
  based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
- You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting
  [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).
- You can also opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized
  advertising at [aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

For more information on how Google uses data, see
[How Google uses information from sites that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## Analytics

If web analytics are enabled, aggregated, anonymized usage data (such as page
views and general location) may be collected to understand how the site is used.
This data is not used to personally identify you.

## Third-party links

This site links to external resources (documentation, tools, GitHub). We are not
responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. Please review
their privacy policies separately.

## Children's privacy

This site is intended for a general, technical audience and is not directed at
children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Your choices

- Disable cookies in your browser settings.
- Opt out of personalized ads via the links above.
- Use browser private/incognito mode to limit stored data.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on
this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

## Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on
[GitHub](https://github.com/Hamza-Afraiz/Python-learning/issues).
